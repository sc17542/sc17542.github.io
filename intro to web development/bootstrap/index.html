Welcome to the world of Pokemon!

Pokemon are creatures of all shapes and sizes and of all different types that can be found in grass, in caves, in the skies, or underwater.

Pokemon Trainers are people that go out on a journey with a Pokemon partner of their own, making new friends with both people and Pokemon and getting stronger along the way.

When Pokemon Trainers are just starting out for the first time, they receive a starter Pokemon from a Pokemon Professor. From there, they officially start their journey.

In most regions, excluding Alola, a Trainer must defeat 8 Gym Leaders in a Pokemon battle and receive 8 Gym Badges in order to enter the Pokemon League. There, they will battle the Elite Four, which consists of 4 powerful Trainers. If they manange to defeat them all, they have a battle with the Champion, the Trainer who had previously defeated the Elite Four. If they manage to win, they become the new Champion of the region.

Kanto

In Kanto, you start off in Pallet Town and receive either a Bulbasaur, Charmander, or Squirtle from Professor Oak.

In Johto, you start off in New Bark Town and receive either a Chikorita, Cyndaquil, or Totodile from Professor Elm.

In Hoenn, you start off in Littleroot Town and receive either a Treecko, Torchic, or Mudkip from Professor Birch.

In Sinnoh, you start off in Twinleaf Town and receive either a Turtwig, Chimchar, or Piplup from Professor Rowan.

In Unova, you start off in Nuvema Town and receive either a Snivy, Tepig, or Oshawott from Professor Juniper.

In Kalos, you start off in Vaniville Town and receive either a Chespin, Fennekin, or Froakie from Professor Sycamore.

In Alola, you start off on Melemele Island and receive either a Rowlet, Litten, or Popplio from Professor Kukui.
