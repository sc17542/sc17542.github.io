Events at the Nest (4/7/19 - 4/13/19)

Posted May 16, 2019

Note: I will start referring to the two adult hawks as Bobby and Aurora on this site.

Apr. 7

(I joined the livestream at 2:56 PM.)

Bobby was on the nest at the time.

At around 4:00 PM, Aurora arrived on the ledge and walked onto the nest. Bobby soon left.

(I left at 5:52 PM. Aurora was still on the nest. I returned at 7:02 PM.)

Bobby was on the nest at the time.

At around 7:24 PM, Aurora arrived. The infrared night cam also turned on at around that time.

Apr. 8

At around 9:18 AM, a chat member noted that Bobby was on the nest. He may have been on the nest since 7:30 AM or even earlier. (I joined the livestream at 12:26 PM temporarily.)

Aurora (I think) was on the nest at the time.

(I joined again at 1:44 PM.)

Aurora was still on the nest (I think).

I checked the cam at 2:36 PM and Bobby was here at that time.

At 3:03 PM, Bobby left. Aurora later arrived.

At 3:57 PM, Bobby arrived and Aurora left.

At 6:49 PM, Aurora arrived on the ledge and climbed onto the nest. Bobby soon left.

The infrared night cam turned on at 7:30 PM.

Apr. 9

(I was busy all day and couldn't check in on the cam.)

At 7:24 PM, a chat member reported that Aurora was here.

At 7:34 PM, a chat member said that Aurora left and Bobby came in.

At 7:43 PM, Aurora came back.

Apr. 10

At 7:08 AM, a chat member reported that Bobby was in the nest. (I joined the livestream at 1:13 PM.)

Aurora was here at the time.

I didn't notice that the livestream had froze for me and after refreshing it at 3:36 PM, Bobby was here.

At 5:42 PM, Aurora arrived on the ledge and walked onto the nest. Bobby left right after.

(I left at 7:19 PM. Aurora was still on the nest at the time.)

Apr. 11

At 6:07 AM, a chat member said that Bobby was here, but Aurora didn't get up from sitting.

At 6:23 AM, a chat member said that Bobby left.

At 7:32 AM, a chat member had talked about Bobby being on the nest.

At 8:55 AM, a chat member had talked about Aurora being on the nest. (I joined the livestream at 12:29 PM.)

Bobby (I think) was on the nest at the time.

At 1:30 PM, Bobby left. He returned at around 1:34 PM to 1:35 PM.

At around 1:38 PM, Aurora arrived. Bobby left at 1:48 PM.

(I left the livestream at 3:26 PM. Aurora was still on at the time.)

Apr. 12

I checked in at 10:00 AM and Aurora was here.

I checked in again at 1:51 PM and Bobby was here.

I checked in once more at 6:51 PM and Aurora was here.

The infrared night cam turned on at 7:23 PM.

Apr. 13

I checked in at 2:05 PM and Aurora was here.

I checked in again at 5:05 PM and Bobby was here.

(All times are reported in the Eastern Time Zone.)
