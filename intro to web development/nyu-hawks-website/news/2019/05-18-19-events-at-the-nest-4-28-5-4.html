Events at the Nest (4/28/19 - 5/4/19)

Posted May 18, 2019

Apr. 28

At 6:05 AM, the chat said that there was a feeding. The food may have been a rat/mouse that Bobby brought.

I joined the livestream at 11:27 AM and Aurora was sitting in the nest bowl. The livestream continued to freeze at times.

At around 12:08 PM, Bobby arrived, possibly with food. At 12:28 PM, the chat said that there was a feeding.

I checked in at 12:41 PM and Aurora was gone. At 12:46 PM, the chat said that Aurora brought a leafy twig to the nest.

At 12:48 PM, Aurora was feeding the eyasses with a rat. She may have left with the rat a minute later. At 12:52 PM, she returned and tried to feed them, but they weren't interested. She went to sit on them at 12:55 PM.

Aurora was preening the eyasses on several occasions, including at 1:08 PM, 1:17 PM, and 1:27 PM.

At around 1:52 PM, Aurora got up. One of the eyasses started pecking one of her siblings. When Aurora turned around to face the nest again, the eyass stopped. The pecking resumed at 2:07 PM. Aurora went to go sit on them at around 2:09 PM to 2:10 PM, but she wasn't covering all of them.

At 2:41 PM, Aurora got up. At around 2:47 PM, she started feeding them. At 2:55 PM, she left with the rat. At 3:02 PM, the chat said that she brought more greenery to the nest.

At 3:31 PM, Aurora got somewhat off of the eyasses and later left at 3:33 PM. At 3:37 PM, one of the eyasses was preening herself.

At 3:41 PM, Bobby arrived. He later left at 3:47 PM. At 3:53 PM, an eyass was preening herself.

I left at 3:53 PM and checked back at 5:25 PM. Aurora was sitting and covering all of the eyasses.

At 6:25 PM, the chat said that Bobby arrived with food.

I checked in again at 6:28 PM and Aurora was feeding them. She went to sit on them at 6:32 PM.

Apr. 29

According to the chat, Aurora fed the eyasses with a rat at around 8 AM. She ate the rest of the rat afterward and left at 8:31 AM.

I quickly checked in at 9:44 AM and Aurora was sitting.

I joined the livestream at 11:56 AM and Aurora was sitting. She got up at 11:58 AM. One of the eyasses may have started to look grayer, moving into her next stage of development. All of the eyasses were sleepy.

At 12:06 PM, Bobby arrived with food, which may or may not have been a pigeon. Aurora took it from him and he left after a few seconds. Aurora was getting ready to feed the eyasses, but they were all asleep.

At 12:12 PM, Aurora was stretching her wings. She moved towards the food at 12:16 PM. She may have started a feeding at 12:18 PM. She took some leftovers, which may have been a wing, and left at 12:19 PM. At 12:22 PM, she returned with a thin branch with leaves on it. One of the eyasses was preening herself at 12:23 PM.

I left at 12:26 PM and returned at 1:14 PM. Bobby had just arrived with a pigeon. He left at 1:15 PM.

I checked in at 1:31 PM and Bobby may have been here.

I checked in at 1:40 PM and Aurora was feeding the eyasses, starting to give them bigger pieces of food. Two of them were getting fed, while the other one was asleep. At one point, she tried to feed them a bigger piece, but it was too big for them to get it down. She also moved to feed the eyass that was lying down. She went to sit on them at 1:50 PM.

At around 2:05 PM, Aurora was preening the eyasses. She got up at 2:10 PM and started stretching at 2:26 PM. She left at 2:32 PM.

At around 2:36 PM, Bobby arrived. Aurora soon arrived after. A minute later, Bobby left and Aurora started rearranging some sticks on the nest.

At 3:10 PM, Bobby arrived and picked up the pigeon on the nest. He flew off with a leftover wing at 3:12 PM.

Sometime before 3:48 PM, Aurora started feeding the eyasses. She started preening them for a bit at 4:15 PM.

At 4:36 PM, Bobby arrived with what may have been a pigeon and later left. Aurora left at 4:38 PM and returned at 4:46 PM. She took some leftover feathers and left at 4:47 PM. She came back at 5:00 PM and started rearranging.

At around 6:53 PM, Aurora was feeding the eyasses with what may have been a pigeon.

At 7:22 PM, Bobby did a quick visit to the nest and soon left.

At 8:56 PM, Aurora was preening the eyasses.

Apr. 30

I was not able to check the livestream at all today.

May 1

According to the chat, the morning feedings happened at 6:30 AM, 8:00 AM, and 9:30 AM.

I joined the livestream at 12:47 PM and Aurora was standing on the nest.

At 1:15 PM, Aurora was stretching.

At 1:32 PM, Aurora flew off the nest with a rat. She returned at 1:44 PM.

At 3:50 PM, Bobby arrived and left three minutes later. At 3:54 PM, Aurora started feeding the eyasses. At 4:04 PM, she left with a rat. She returned at 4:11 PM.

At 4:26 PM, Aurora started feeding the eyasses again. At 4:47 PM, she left with a rat and came back at 5:01 PM.

At around 5:29 PM, Aurora started preening an eyass.

At 6:22 PM, Aurora left, Bobby arrived, and Aurora returned. At 6:23 PM, Bobby left and soon after, Aurora left with him. At 6:29 PM, Aurora returned. She may have started a feeding at 6:30 PM. At 6:31 PM, she left with what may have been a rat. At 6:33 PM, she returned with food and may have started feeding the eyasses again.

May 2

At 8:45 AM, the chat said that one of the hawks brought in a new paper material with a barcode on it.

At 10:47 AM, the chat said that Aurora was gone for about 20 minutes.

I joined the livestream at 11:49 AM. Bobby and Aurora were both here. Bobby soon left.

At 11:59 AM, Aurora left. She returned at 12:06 PM.

At 12:17 PM, Aurora left. At 12:23 PM, she returned with a rat. She soon started feeding the eyasses with it. At one point, one of the eyasses was having trouble getting down a long strand. Aurora left with the rat at 12:32 PM. The eyass continued to have trouble getting down the long piece. The other two eyasses both watched their sibling struggle with getting the piece down. At one point, they tried pulling at the piece. At 12:36 PM, the eyass continued to have trouble with that long piece of food. One of the other two was gently poking at the eyass having trouble.

At 1:00 PM, Aurora and Bobby arrived with food. Bobby soon left. Aurora started feeding the eyasses.

At 2:09 PM, Aurora left. At 2:21 PM, she returned with food. She started feeding the eyasses with a pigeon. At 2:49 PM, she left with the pigeon. She returned at 2:52 PM.

At 3:14 PM, Bobby arrived with food and left at 3:16 PM. At 3:20 PM, Aurora took some leftovers and left. At around 3:30 PM, she returned and started a feeding. At 3:32 PM, she left with a pigeon and returned at 3:44 PM.

At 4:46 PM, Bobby arrived and left at 4:49 PM.

At 4:52 PM, Aurora left. She returned with a leafy twig at 4:56 PM.

At 5:32 PM, Aurora left and returned at 5:35 PM.

At 6:00 PM, Aurora left and later came back. Sometime before 6:08 PM, she started feeding the eyasses. At 6:34 PM, she took some leftovers and left. All of the eyasses seemed to be full.

At 6:38 PM, Aurora returned. She picked up a leftover on the nest and tried to offer it to the eyasses, but they were all sleepy. She was persistent in trying to give the leftover food to them. At 6:42 PM, one of them took it from her. She continued to feed the eyass some of the leftovers until 6:45 PM. At 6:46 PM, she took some of the leftovers and ate it herself. At 6:47 PM, she picked up some other leftovers and dropped it. At 6:48 PM, she tried to move a branch on the nest before going to sit on them.

May 3

At 6:36 AM, the chat said that a squirrel was on the nest.

At 6:48 AM, the chat had mentioned Bobby, which may have meant that he was on the nest.

At 8:46 AM, based on the chat messages, both parents may have been on the nest.

I checked in at 9:42 AM and Aurora was feeding the eyasses with the squirrel. She took the squirrel tail and left at 9:43 AM. At 9:57 AM, she returned with a leafy branch and placed it on the nest. She tried to sit on the eyasses at 9:59 AM.

I joined the livestream at 2:16 PM and the eyasses were alone on the nest.

At 3:20 PM, Aurora arrived. She left at 3:22 PM. At 3:23 PM, Bobby arrived. At 3:24 PM, Aurora returned, Bobby left, and then Aurora soon left with him. At 3:31 PM, Aurora returned with food and started feeding the eyasses.

I left at 3:39 PM and returned at 4:40 PM. The eyasses were alone on the nest.

At 4:41 PM, Bobby arrived with what may have been a pigeon. Aurora soon arrived after. At 4:43 PM, she started feeding the eyasses with what Bobby brought. At 4:45 PM, Bobby left.

At 5:14 PM, Aurora flew off with some leftovers and returned at 5:21 PM. At 5:23 PM, she flew off with some more leftovers and returned sometime before 5:32 PM. At 5:57 PM, she flew off with another leftover.

At 6:10 PM, Bobby arrived with a rat. Aurora returned soon after. At 6:12 PM, Bobby left. Aurora started feeding the eyasses with the rat.

At 6:31 PM, Aurora took the rat and flew off. She returned at 6:33 PM.

May 4

At 8:50 AM, the chat said that the eyasses were alone on the nest.

At 8:57 AM, the chat said that food was brought in and that there was some attempted self-feeding by one of the eyasses.

I checked in at 9:42 AM and Aurora was standing over the eyasses.

I joined the livestream at 10:52 AM and Aurora was standing.

At 12:10 PM, Aurora left and returned at around 12:14 PM.

At 12:46 PM, Aurora was feeding the eyasses with what may have been a pigeon. She stopped the feeding at 1:03 PM. She left at 1:04 PM.

At around 1:20 PM, Bobby arrived. He left at 1:23 PM. At 1:27 PM, Aurora returned. At around 1:30 PM, she left.

I left at 1:58 PM and rejoined at 3:06 PM. There was a feeding in progress.

At 3:10 PM, Aurora left. She returned at 3:21 PM and started rearranging sticks on the nest. She left at 3:31 PM.

At 3:59 PM, Bobby arrived and Aurora soon arrived as well. Bobby left at 4:00 PM. At 4:05 PM, Aurora may have been feeding the eyasses. At 4:19 PM, she took the leftovers and flew off. She returned at 4:25 PM.

At 5:35 PM, Aurora left.

At 6:34 PM, Bobby arrived with food and Aurora soon arrived after. Bobby soon left. At 6:43 PM, Aurora may have started feeding the eyasses.

At 7:26 PM, Bobby arrived with food and soon left.

At 7:57 PM, the infrared night cam turned on.

(All times are reported in the Eastern Time Zone.)
