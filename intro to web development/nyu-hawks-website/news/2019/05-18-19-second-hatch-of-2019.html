Second Hatch of 2019!

Posted May 18, 2019

On April 23, 2019, the second hatch of the 2019 nesting season occurred.

It happened early in the morning, much like the first hatch. No one is certain of the exact hatch time, but it was sometime before 6:45 AM.

Due to the uncertainty in the time of the hatch, there is no video available for the second hatch.


(All times are reported in the Eastern Time Zone.)
