Halloween Safety

It's Halloween night and you can't wait to go trick-or-treating. However, before you go out, make sure you follow these tips to keep yourself safe.

Tips for Staying Safe on Halloween

  • Pay attention when crossing the street.
  • If possible, avoid wearing masks that block any part of your vision.
  • Carry a glow stick or a flashlight or put reflective tape on your costume to make sure drivers can see you at night.
  • Wear costumes that won't cause you to trip and fall.
  • Trick-or-treat with family members or friends instead of going alone.
  • Never go inside a stranger's home, even if they say the candy is inside.
  • Trick-or-treat in neighborhoods that you're familiar with.
  • Check the candy or have your parents check the candy before you eat them to see if they're safe to eat.
