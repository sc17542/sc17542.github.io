Who We Are

The name of our company says it all. We are party planners. That's just who we are.

It started a couple years ago when we were planning out some birthday parties for our friends. For every one of them, we planned everything out to the last detail to make sure the whole thing was perfect. Seeing everybody have such a great time made us want to do more parties. But our friends' birthdays only come around every so often. What would we do for the rest of the year?

That's when we got the idea to help other people plan their parties.

Not everyone knows how to throw a great party. And that's okay. That's where we come in.

We can help you out at any stage of the party planning process, whether you're nearly finished or just starting out. We help you with setting up all the decorations, preparing the food, and anything else you may need for your party. If you need a venue for your party, we can help with that too.

Party planning is our passion and we feel that it wouldn't be right to overcharge you for wanting to have a great time with your family and friends. That's why we do all of this with the minimum amount of cost to you. Now, you can finally plan that big party and save money while you're at it!

Everybody loves parties, but not everybody loves party planning. With PartyPlanners, you don't have to stress about it anymore. After all, it's a party. And we feel that every party planner should have as fun of a time as any party guest does.
