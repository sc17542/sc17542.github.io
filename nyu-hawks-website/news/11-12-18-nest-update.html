11/12/18 Nest Update

As of November 12, the work at Bobst Library continues to progress. If you have tuned into the hawk cam recently, you will notice that the work is sometimes visible on the cam. In the picture shown above, you can see one of the workers at the window next to the one with the nest.

It is not known whether or not the two adult hawks have visited the nest recently, but according to viewers who have been at Washington Square Park, they have been spotted in the area on multiple occasions. They were even seen chasing out a young juvenile hawk from the park, a sign indicating that they are continuing to defend this territory from others.
