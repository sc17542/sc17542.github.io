Third Egg of 2019!

Posted Mar. 22, 2019 (Updated Mar. 29, 2019)

On March 21, 2019, the female hawk laid the third egg in the 2019 nesting season.

Around 2 PM, the female hawk was getting up several times while she was on the nest. When she got up at around 2:08 PM, there were three eggs in the nest.

Watch the video of the third egg laying on the NYU Hawk Cam's Ustream page!
