Hawk Visits on 1/5/19

Posted Jan. 5, 2019

At 9:42 AM, a chat member reported seeing the male hawk Bobby visit the nest, sitting and also moving the turquoise material on the nest. He was said to have left sometime before 10:56 AM.

The chat member also reported seeing the female hawk Aurora visit the nest for a few minutes, sitting and also moving the turquoise material on the nest.
