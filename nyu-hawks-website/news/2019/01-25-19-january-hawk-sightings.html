Hawk Visits in January 2019

Posted Jan. 25, 2019 (Updated Feb. 5, 2019)

Jan. 4

At 7:41 AM, the male hawk was spotted on the nest, both sitting and standing. He left at 7:58 AM.

Jan. 5

At 9:42 AM, the male hawk was on the nest, sitting and also moving the turquoise material around. He left sometime before 10:56 AM. The female hawk also visited the nest at some point and did the same things that her mate did.

Jan. 6

At 7:30 AM, one of the hawks was spotted on the nest.

Jan. 8

The male hawk made two long nest visits, while the female hawk made one shorter nest visit. They reportedly worked on the nest and moved some fabric around.

Jan. 10

At 12:45 PM, one of the hawks visited the nest.

Jan. 12

At 1:06 PM, a hawk was seen on the nest, although the visit began sometime before that. It left at 1:07 PM.

Jan. 18

At 9:47 AM, a hawk was spotted on the nest. It flew off at 9:49 AM. It came back at 9:56 AM and then left again at 10:00 AM. The hawk was thought to have been the male.

Jan. 20

At 7:19 AM, the male hawk was seen on the nest.

Jan. 23

At 7:39 AM, both of the adult hawks were on the nest. At 7:42 AM, the female hawk left. The male hawk was reportedly moving around some twigs on the nest. He flew off at 7:46 AM.

Jan. 24

At 8:46 AM, the male hawk was on the nest. He left at 9:31 AM.

Jan. 25

At 10:11 AM, the male hawk was seen on the nest. At 10:12 AM, both of the hawks were seen on the nest. At 10:13 AM, the male hawk left. A couple seconds after, the female hawk left.

Jan. 26

At 4:36 PM, the male hawk flew onto the nest. A few seconds later, the female hawk flew onto the nest. The male hawk soon left while the female hawk stayed to adjust some things on the nest. She left at 4:38 PM.

Jan. 27

At 8:18 AM, the male hawk was on the nest, moving around some twigs. At 8:25 AM, the female hawk arrived with a twig while the male hawk left. At 8:32 AM, the female hawk left.

Jan. 30

At 8:06 AM, the male hawk visited the nest.


Thank you to all of the chat members that got up early to watch the hawk cam and reported any visits that occurred!

This list may not contain all the visits that happened over the course of the month (some visits may go unreported and some will be missed), but these are the ones that I saw being reported in the NYU Hawk Cam Ustream chatroom and one that I saw myself.

(All times are reported in the Eastern Time Zone.)
