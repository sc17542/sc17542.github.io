Reported Hawk Sightings on 11/28/18

Two hawk cam chat members commented that they saw a hawk on the nest, possibly on two separate occasions.

One chat member reported a visit by the male hawk Bobby at about 7:57 AM and noted that he was moving around the sticks on the nest.

There may have been another visit sometime earlier or later in the morning.
