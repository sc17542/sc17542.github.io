Adult Hawks Visit the Nest Multiple Times on 11/20/18

The two adult hawks were spotted on the nest cam multiple times today.

One sighting reported by a hawk cam chat moderator was reported at 9:55 AM. Another chat member reported that there was a visit prior to that one also.

I happened to check in on the hawk cam right as a hawk landed onto the nest. That visit was at around 10:27 AM. The female hawk Aurora fixed the nest a bit and looked out to the park. She left at about 10:33 AM.

Not long after, the male hawk Bobby flew in and adjusted a few sticks in the nest before flying off at 10:34 AM.
