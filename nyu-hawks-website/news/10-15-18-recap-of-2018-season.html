Recap of the 2018 Season

The male hawk Bobby and the female hawk Aurora returned for the 2018 season.

Aurora laid three eggs in total: one on March 24, one on March 27, and one on March 30.

All three eggs hatched. Two of them hatched on April 30, one at around 7:44 AM and one at around 10:26 PM. The third one hatched on May 3 at around 7:12 AM.

All three eyasses fledged successfully. The first fledge was on June 16. The second fledge was on June 19. The third fledge was on June 21.
