Reported Sightings of Hawks at the Nest on 10/16/18

Today, there were some reported sightings of the adult hawks returning to the nest. However, the hawk cam footage is too blurry to confirm which hawk was which.

There may be more instances where they have shown up on the webcam, but these are the ones that were reported:

At 8:41 AM, one of the NYU hawk cam chat members commented that they saw what they thought was the male hawk Bobby placing a stick onto the nest.

At 12:11 PM, I saw a hawk on the nest, possibly the male hawk, standing and looking out to Washington Square Park.

At 12:19 PM, the other hawk flew in and joined its partner on the nest for a short amount of time before taking off again.

At 12:21 PM, the remaining hawk flew away from the nest.

At 12:26 PM, one of the hawks returned and quickly adjusted one of the sticks before flying off about a minute later.
